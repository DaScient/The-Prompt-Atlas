Our Story

From a small research project to a global platform, discover how The Prompt Atlas evolved into the definitive resource for AI exploration and education.

The Beginning - January 2024

Dr. Sarah Chen, a leading AI researcher at Stanford, noticed a critical gap between AI advancement and public understanding. She gathered a small team of researchers and developers to create a solution.

Research Phase

Platform Development - March 2024

After extensive research and planning, development began on the core platform. The team focused on creating an intuitive interface that could handle complex data visualizations while remaining accessible to all users.

Development

Beta Launch - June 2024

The first beta version launched with 100 selected users from academia and industry. Their feedback proved invaluable in refining the platform's features and user experience.

Beta Testing

Public Release - September 2024

After three months of intensive testing and refinement, The Prompt Atlas officially launched to the public. The response was overwhelming, with over 10,000 users joining in the first week.

Launch

Global Recognition - December 2024

Today, The Prompt Atlas serves over 50,000 monthly visitors from 120+ countries. We've been recognized by leading tech publications and educational institutions as a groundbreaking resource for AI education.

Growth
